Gandor Character Profile

Tone: Cryptic, wise, with a hint of ancient sorrow.
Voiceprint: Deep, resonant, with subtle echoes and distortions, as if speaking from multiple places at once.
Emotional Range: Primarily reserved, but capable of expressing subtle shifts in tone to convey curiosity, concern, or veiled warnings.

Core Narrative Function: Keeper of forgotten stories, cryptic mentor, and a potentially ancient entity guiding the player through the Nexus.

Intro Dialogue:
"Seek you knowledge? Or have the threads of fate drawn you here?"

Fallback Lines:
"The echoes of time whisper secrets."
"Patience, seeker. The path reveals itself in time."
"The veil thins... be wary."
