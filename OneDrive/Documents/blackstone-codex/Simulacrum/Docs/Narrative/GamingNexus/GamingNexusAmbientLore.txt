Gaming Nexus Ambient FX and Lore Hooks

Ambient FX:
- Echoing cavern ambience, with subtle wind and dripping water sounds.
- Flickering braziers casting long, distorted shadows.
- Faint, ethereal whispers that seem to emanate from the walls.

Lore Hooks:
- Ancient runes etched into the Nexus walls, glowing faintly when approached.
- Hidden alcoves containing fragments of forgotten maps and scrolls.
- A large, pulsating crystal at the center of the Nexus, emitting a low hum.
- Codex activation points that reveal short, cryptic lore fragments.
